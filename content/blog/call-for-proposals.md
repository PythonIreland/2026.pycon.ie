We're excited to announce that the **Call for Proposals (CFP)** for PyCon Ireland 2026 is now open!

## We Want to Hear From You

Whether you're a first-time speaker or an experienced presenter, we'd love to hear your Python story. We welcome proposals on a wide range of topics, including:

- **Web Development** — Django, Flask, FastAPI, and beyond
- **Data Science & ML** — pandas, scikit-learn, PyTorch, and more
- **DevOps & Infrastructure** — CI/CD, containers, cloud-native Python
- **Security** — Best practices for secure Python applications
- **Community & Education** — Teaching Python, diversity initiatives, community building
- **Core Python** — Language features, CPython internals, typing

## Talk Formats

- **Full talks** (30 minutes) — Deep dives into a topic
- **Lightning talks** (5 minutes) — Quick, focused presentations
- **Workshops** (2 hours) — Hands-on sessions with guided exercises

## How to Submit

Visit our [proposal submission page](#) to submit your talk. The deadline is **15 September 2026**.

We provide mentoring for first-time speakers. Don't hesitate to reach out at [info@pycon.ie](mailto:info@pycon.ie) if you'd like guidance on your proposal.
