We're thrilled to announce that **PyCon Ireland 2026** will take place on **17 October 2026** at **Trinity College Dublin**.

After years of building Ireland's Python community, we're bringing together the best speakers, workshops, and networking opportunities for Python developers of all levels.

## What to Expect

- **50+ talks** across multiple tracks covering web development, data science, machine learning, DevOps, and more
- **Hands-on workshops** for beginners and advanced practitioners
- **Lightning talks** where anyone can share their Python story
- **Networking events** to connect with fellow Pythonistas

## Call for Proposals

Our Call for Proposals will open soon. Whether you're a first-time speaker or a seasoned presenter, we want to hear from you. Stay tuned for more details.

- **Sponsor:** Support Ireland's largest Python event
- **Volunteer:** Help us make PyCon Ireland amazing
