We are committed to making PyCon Ireland 2026 accessible to everyone. If you have specific requirements, please contact us in advance.

{{/* Getting there */}}

Getting Here

  • Luas Green Line (recommended): Trinity stop on Pearse Street — 3 min walk to Nassau Gate. Fully step-free with level boarding.
  • DART: Pearse Station — ~5 min walk to Lincoln Gate. Has lifts; check status on the day.
  • Dublin Bus: 100% low-floor fleet. Stops on Nassau Street and College Green.
  • Blue badge parking: ~10 disabled bays on campus. Enter via Lincoln Gate (Lincoln Place) and ask security for the nearest bay. First-come, no pre-booking.
{{/* Campus access */}}

Campus Access

  • Recommended entrance: Lincoln Gate (Lincoln Place) or Nassau Street Gate — both step-free.
  • Accessible toilets throughout campus, including a Changing Places facility (hoist, height-adjustable bench) in the Arts Block — key available from security.
  • Induction loops available in lecture rooms. We will confirm which conference rooms have loops once the programme is finalised.
  • Guide and assistance dogs are welcome throughout the entire campus and all conference spaces.
